About US

AV Sense

Audio Video Personified is not just a slogan for us, but we truly endeavour to achieve that tag-Line.

AV Sense is a national distributor of some of the finest speaker brands from Europe. Indiana-Line from Italy, Audium from Germany, AQ Audio from the Czech Republic and Casta from Italy.

AV-Sense has chosen these brands to capture the imagination of Its prospective clients for the right reasons. Be it sonic or aesthetic, or to fit the desired budget without compromising the sound.

AV Sense as well as being a national distributor for premium speaker systems is also a company that provides AV integration services par excellence. We offer our clients end-to-end solutions with all aspects of AV requirements from products to installation and post-sales service.

We work with our clients to achieve their audiovisual goals. We guide them through the process of product selection, associated components, and accessories along the chain. AV Sense also provides all kinds of acoustical treatment of all audiovisual spaces, like Home theatres, Studios, boardrooms, auditoriums, and pure stereo music rooms.
