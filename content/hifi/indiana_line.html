Indiana Line

DIVA Series

Diva series loudspeakers provide the performance required to meet the fidelity, dynamic and power handling required by the latest recording techniques in order to recreate the exciting emotions of a live concert or special effects of an action movie in your listening environment. But, most of all, they are mainly defined by the enviable definition and naturalness of voice reproduction, with fast and accurate transient response, with warm and full-bodied low frequencies to which the natural and detailed high frequencies are added. The Diva loudspeakers are finished with an elegant high gloss black lacquer.

TESI Series

The high definition and naturalness of voice reproduction, combined with fast and precise transient response, characterize all the speakers in the Tesi series. The tweeters use very light silk domes, powerful neodymium magnets and an exclusive rear resonance chamber. The mid-woofer are equipped with mica-reinforced polypropylene cones and central phase plugs to improve mid-frequency response and dispersion. The accurate white high gloss lacquering makes the models of this line precious.

NOTA-X Series

The Nota-X series loudspeakers provide the performance needed to meet the sound quality, dynamic level and power handling required by the most advanced recording techniques, in order to recreate in your listening room the engaging emotions of a live concert as well as the sound effects of an action movie. An optimized production process and a perfect blend of all the components brought to life this range of loudspeakers with a very good price/performance ratio.

DJ Series

DJ 308 and DJ 310, with their high dynamics, are the perfect complement to digital sources: the ideal solution to listen to music in the basement, dance school or gym. The front waveguide of the domHorn tweeter provides excellent polar dispersion along with high efficiency the rear resonance chamber and the treated silk dome guarantee high transients speed and crystal clear high frequencies. DJ loudspeakers are finished in black ash vinyl.

SUBWOOFERS

Basso models are active subwoofers with an amazing acoustic impact. They are born to complement the low-frequency extension of loudspeakers used in hi-fi systems and home cinema systems. Their amplifiers are equipped with a very complete control section, which allows a perfect interface with other components of the audio system and the listening room. The clean, refined design of the chassis and the compact size make it easy to insert them into any room.

LUNA | NANO

Luna and Nanoare 2-way loudspeakers suitable for different types of environments, including shops, bars, restaurants and private homes. Both are provided with special brackets that allow quick and easy wall mounting with the ability to choose the angle. The elegant and clean design makes them suitable for a discreete placement in every kind of furniture. In addition, Luna box is made with materials resistant to moisture and sun allowing even for an outdoor installation.

IN-WALL | IN-CEILING

The in-wall/in-ceiling loudspeakers are suitable for hi-fi stereo systems and home cinema, surround sound effects and sound coverage of even large environments. All models can be easily installed on walls, drywalls or plasterboard ceilings, without the use of additional accessories. To ease installation on brick walls, Tesi Q is equipped with a special box to be embedded in the solid wall.
